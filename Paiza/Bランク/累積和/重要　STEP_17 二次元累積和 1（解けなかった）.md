## 模範解答
```python
a = [[i+j for i in range(1,6)]for j in range(5)]
s = [[0] * 6 for _ in range(6)]

for i in range(1,6):
    for j in range(1,6):
        s[i][j] = s[i][j-1] + s[i-1][j] - s[i-1][j-1] + a[i-1][j-1]

print(s[4][4] - s[1][4] - s[4][1] + s[1][1])
```
